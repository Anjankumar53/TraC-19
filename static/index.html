TraC-19

TraC-19 aims to provide its users with all around information pertaining to the COVID-19 pandemic. Besides viewing statistics for any country in the world, the website offers its users quick access to time critical information such as bed availability in hospitals. The TraC-19 Chatbot allows users to analyse their medical condition by answering a series of questions following which they receive advice based on their answers.
